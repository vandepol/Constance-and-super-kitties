Deep in The Cavern, two little kitties — Ginny and Bitsy — are hunting for cat treats to fill their basket. Fill it up to suit up as Super Kitties! A sneaky Cat Burglar wants the treats too — press SPACE to throw yarn and slow her down!

How to play

🎮 Move with the ← ↑ ↓ → arrow keys (or WASD, or the buttons).

🧶 Press SPACE (or the 🧶 button) to throw yarn and slow down the baddie.

Level 1 — The Cavern: 🐟 catch cat treats to fill your basket (gold ones = 3!). Beat the Cat Burglar to them. Full basket → SUIT UP!

Level 2 — Kitty Land: 🐶 Mr. Puppy Paws wants the cat toys! Chase him and hit him with yarn (SPACE) again and again until he's all tangled up. Push the wooden crates out of your way if you need to!
